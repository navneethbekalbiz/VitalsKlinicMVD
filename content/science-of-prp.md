## What is PRP?

Platelet-Rich Plasma (PRP) therapy utilizes your body's own healing mechanisms to rejuvenate skin and stimulate hair follicles.

### The Procedure
1. **Draw**: A small amount of blood is drawn.
2. **Spin**: It is centrifuged to separate the plasma.
3. **Inject**: The concentrated platelets are injected into the scalp.
