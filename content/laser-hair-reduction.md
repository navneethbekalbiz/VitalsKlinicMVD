### **What is Laser Hair Reduction?**

Laser Hair Reduction is a medical procedure that uses concentrated beams of light to destroy hair follicles, preventing future growth.

#### **How Does It Work?**
- A laser emits light absorbed by the pigment (melanin) in the hair.
- The light energy is converted to heat, damaging the hair follicles.
- This damage inhibits or delays future hair growth.

### **Types of Lasers Used**

At **Vitalsklinic**, we use FDA-approved technology including:
- **Diode Laser** – Suitable for most skin types.
- **Alexandrite Laser** – Ideal for lighter skin tones.
- **Nd:YAG Laser** – Effective on darker skin tones.

Each laser is tailored to your unique hair and skin type to ensure optimal results.

### **The Pros and Cons**

#### **Pros**
1.  **Precision:** Lasers can target dark, coarse hairs while leaving the surrounding skin undamaged.
2.  **Speed:** Each pulse of the laser takes a fraction of a second and can treat many hairs at the same time. Small areas like the upper lip can be treated in less than a minute.
3.  **Predictability:** Most patients have permanent hair loss after an average of three to seven sessions.

#### **Cons**
1.  **Multiple Sessions Required:** It's not a one-time fix. You need patience.
2.  **Cost:** Higher upfront cost compared to razors, but cheaper in the long run.
3.  **Sun Sensitivity:** You must avoid sun exposure before and after treatment to prevent pigmentation issues.
